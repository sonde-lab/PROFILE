BABOLAT

Babolat is a French tennis, badminton, and squash equipment company,
headquartered in Lyon, best known for its strings and tennis racquets which are used by several top players.
The company has made strings since 1875, when Pierre Babolat created the first strings made of natural gut.




WILSON

Wilson rackets are tennis rackets manufactured by Wilson Sporting Goods,a major player in the tennis equipment industry.
They offer a wide range of racket models catering to various skill levels and playing styles,
with different constructions, head sizes, and string patterns designed to optimize performance.


HEAD

"Head rackets" generally refers to the balance and weight distribution of a racket,
specifically the amount of weight concentrated at the head.
A head-heavy racket has more weight towards the head, while a head-light racket has more weight towards
the handle. This affects how the racket feels when swung and how it handles different shots.
