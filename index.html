Babolat is a French tennis, badminton, and squash equipment company, headquartered in Lyon, best known for its strings and tennis racquets which are used by several top players.

WILSON

A Wilson tennis racket is a tennis racket manufactured by Wilson Sporting Goods, a well-known and respected brand in the tennis world.

HEAD

In the context of tennis, "head size" refers to the area of the racquet where the strings are strung. It's measured in square inches and significantly impacts a player's power, control, and maneuverability.
